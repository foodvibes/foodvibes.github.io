Hunter's Sandwich

On one side of each slice of bread, spread mustard and ranch then sprinkle with salt and pepper. Layer greens, cheese, salami, chips and more greens between the two slices of bread.

Serve immediately with a side of chips.

2 multi-grain bread slices mustard ranch dressing salt black pepper mixed salad greens 1 cheese slice 8 salami slices taro chips Makes one sandwich.
