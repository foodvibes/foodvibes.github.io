Mac Salad

Whisk milk, mayonnaise, sugar, salt and pepper in a bowl until sugar is dissolved. Set aside.

Cook macaroni until very soft. Drain macaroni and add vinegar while still hot. Toss until absorbed. Let cool for 10 minutes then add dressing, carrots, celery and a salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle furikake on top and refrigerate for one hour until dressing sets.

Serve chilled.

2 cups milk 2 cups mayonnaise 1 tablespoon brown sugar ½ teaspoon salt 2 teaspoons black pepper 1 pound elbow macaroni ½ cup coconut vinegar 1 carrot, diced 1 celery stalk, diced furikake Makes 10 servings of mac salad.
