Sunbutter Cookies

Preheat oven to degrees. Combine the oil, butter, honey and sugar, egg and vanilla in a large mixing bowl. Mix in the flour, baking powder, baking soda and sunflower kernels to form dough. Place tablespoon-sized portions onto an oiled cookie sheet and bake at 375°F for 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Serve warm or let cool and store.

½ cup coconut oil ½ cup sunflower seed butter ½ cup honey ½ cup brown sugar 1 egg ½ teaspoon vanilla 1¼ cup flour ½ teaspoon baking powder ½ teaspoon baking soda ½ cup sunflower kernels, roasted Makes 12 cookies.
