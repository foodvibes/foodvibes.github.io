Uisce Anann

Combine equal parts whiskey, sour mix and pineapple juice with a splash of soda to preference. Pour over ice.

Serve in a tumbler.

1 ounce irish whiskey 1 ounce sour mix 1 ounce pineapple juice soda Makes one drink.
