Checksmix

Mix butter, worcestershire, hot sauce, salt, garlic and onion powders in a small bowl. Pour over mixture of cereal and crackers in an extra large mixing bowl. Sprinkle with furikake and mix well. Spread mixture in a thin layer on a large baking sheet and bake at 250°F for an hour, turning every 15 minutes.

Serve immediately or let cool and store.

½ cup butter, melted 4 tablespoons worcestershire 4 teaspoons hot sauce 2 teaspoons salt 1½ teaspoon garlic powder ½ teaspoon onion powder 8 cups of mixed grain cereal 2 cups cheese crackers furikake Makes 10 cups of snack mix.
