Dirty Blondies

In a mixing bowl, combine the butter and sugar. Mix well. Add the vanilla, egg and salt and mix until smooth. Add the flour and mix until incorporated. Pour batter into a buttered 10″ baking pan and bake at 350°F for 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean. Cut into squares.

Serve warm with a scoop of your favorite ice cream.

½ cup unsalted butter, melted 1 cup brown sugar 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract 1 egg salt 1 cup flour Makes 12 blondies.
