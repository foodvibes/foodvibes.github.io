Dharmic Salad

Add mixed greens to a large bowl. Mix in sprouts, beets, carrots, cucumber and tomatoes. Add croutons, sprinkle with sunflower kernels and cheese. Drizzle ranch all over and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Serve immediately.

3 ounces mixed salad greens ¼ cup broccoli sprouts ½ roasted red beet, shredded ½ large carrot, shredded ½ cucumber, sliced ½ heirloom tomato, diced croutons roasted sunflower kernels parmesan cheese ranch dressing salt black pepper Makes one salad.
