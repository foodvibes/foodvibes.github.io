Pancakes

Whisk milk and egg together and set aside. In separate bowl combine flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. Combine the butter, milk and flour mixtures and whisk together. Pour ¼ cup of batter into a lightly oiled, preheated skillet, cooking over medium heat until golden brown on each side.

Serve warm with butter and syrup.

1¼ cups milk 1 egg 1½ cups cake flour 3½ teaspoons baking powder ¼ cup sugar ½ teaspoon salt 3 tablespoons butter, melted Makes 8 pancakes.
