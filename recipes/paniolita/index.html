Paniolita

Combine tequila, triple sec, pineapple juice and lime juice. Mix well and pour over ice.

Serve in li hing mui-rimmed tumbler.

1 ounce tequila blanco 1 ounce triple sec 2 ounces pineapple juice 1 lime, juiced li hing mui powder Makes one drink.
