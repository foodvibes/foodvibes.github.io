Pico de Gallo

Combine everything including salt and pepper to taste in a bowl and mix well. Refrigerate for 10 minutes to let flavors set.

Serve chilled with tortilla chips.

1 tomato, diced 1 white onion, diced ½ fresh jalapeño, diced ½ lime, juiced ¼ cup cilantro, chopped 1 garlic clove, chopped ¼ teaspoon cumin salt black pepper Makes four servings of salsa.
