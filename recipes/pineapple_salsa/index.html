Pineapple Salsa

Combine everything including salt to taste in a bowl and mix well. Refrigerate for 10 minutes to let flavors set.

Serve chilled with taro chips.

2 cups pineapple, diced ½ cup cilantro, chopped ¼ cup red onion, chopped 1 serrano pepper, chopped 1 lime, juiced salt Makes four servings of salsa.
