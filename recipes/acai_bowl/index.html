Açaí Bowl

Cut all fruit into bite sized pieces and set aside. Blend frozen açaí berries with hemp milk and pour into a bowl. Layer granola on top of açaí mixture. Layer fruit on top of granola. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with pollen.

Serve immediately.

¾ cup frozen açaí berries ¼ cup hemp milk 1 cup granola ½ banana ½ kiwi ½ cup strawberries ½ cup blueberries ½ cup pineapple honey bee pollen Makes one bowl.
