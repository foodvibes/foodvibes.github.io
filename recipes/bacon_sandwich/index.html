Bacon Sandwich

Cook bacon to preference in a preheated skillet over medium heat and set aside on a paper towel to absorb some of the grease. Cook eggs to preference in the same skillet using the existing bacon grease. At the same time, toast the bread and spread the dressing on each side. Layer bacon, eggs, cheese and arugula inside bread.

Serve immediately.

3 bacon slices 2 eggs 2 bread slices ranch dressing 1 cheese slice arugula Makes one sandwich.
