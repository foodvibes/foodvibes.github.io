Breakfast Tacos

Cook bacon to preference in a preheated skillet over medium heat and set aside on a paper towel to absorb some of the grease. Cook eggs to preference in the same skillet using the existing bacon grease. Wrap eggs, bacon and arugula in warm tortillas. Sprinkle with cheese.

Serve with salsa.

2 bacon slices 2 eggs arugula 2 corn tortillas jack cheese, shredded Makes two tacos.
