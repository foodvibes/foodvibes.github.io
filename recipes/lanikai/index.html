Lanikai

Combine rum with curaçao, pineapple juice, lime juice and coconut syrup. Mix well and pour over ice.

Serve in a tumbler garnished with a lime or pineapple wedge.

1 ounce white rum 1 ounce curaçao 1 ounce pineapple juice 1 lime, juiced 1 tablespoon coconut syrup Makes one drink.
